About

NCT Dream is the third sub-unit of the South Korean boy band NCT,
formed by SM Entertainment in 2016. They were initially intended
to be the teenaged unit of NCT with an admission-and-graduation system
in which members would leave after reaching age of majority, prior to
re-branding in 2020.
